How to Play

  1. Bet an amount
  2. Press New Game
  3. look at your values and determine if you want to add a new number, if so press Add Card
  4. When you do not want to add a new card anymore press Show Bots to see the bots values ( if they amount below 16 press Show Bots button again)
  5. Once you can not press show bots anymore press End Game and the game will end, your balance will change accordingly

Rules

  • Try to get as close to 21 as possible while staying below 21
  • make sure your values amount to over the bots though
  • DO NOT LOSE ALL OF YOUR MONEY

How to Win

  1. your numbers amount to more than bots and below 21
  2. The bot's numbers go over 21
